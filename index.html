Biography

Hey folks!

I am Amrit Singhal. I am a final year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur.

My broad fields of interest are Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Artifical Intelligence. Recently, I have also developed a new found interest towards Neurobiolgy and its computational aspects, and I am currently exploring this area. Apart from the technical aspects, I am also interested towards Sociology in general, and I love to understand how it helps us make sense of our society.

I was a Research Intern at the Big Data Experience Lab, Adobe Systems, Bangalore, where I worked in the area of Augmented Reality. I have also interned at Hike Pvt. Ltd, New Delhi, where I worked towards developing a Language Independant Text-to-Emotion Classifier. Apart from this, I have done numerous projects on areas like Cross Modal Media Retrieval, Neural Machine Translation, Abstractive Document Summarisation and others. An exhaustive detailed list of my projects can be found in the Projects Section.

In my free time, I love reading novels, and listening to music (like every other human on this planet :P). I am also a gaming enthusiast, playing numerous e-sports from time to time.

Cross Modal Media Retrieval

Neural Machine Translation for Low Resource Languages

Performed an extensive literature review on the existing methods of Neural Machine Translation using parallel and non-parallel corpora. Implemented the state-of-the-art work using non-parallel corpora on low resource language Catalan and performed analysis of results obtained to explain the possible differences arising on using a low resource language.

Quantum Machine Learning

Studied two of the most widely used machine learning strategies, namely K-Nearest Neighbours algorithm and Perceptron Learning algorithm, in a quantum setting, and study the speedups that the quantum modules allow over the classical counterparts.
